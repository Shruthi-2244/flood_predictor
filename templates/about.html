System Architecture & Science

How AquaGuard AI integrates meteorological datasets with predictive models.

Problem Statement

The Flood Mitigation Challenge

Extreme rainfall occurrences driven by monsoonal fluctuations pose an escalating risk to lives, agriculture, and urban infrastructure. Traditional forecasting systems rely on raw radar metrics that often miss localized topographical interactions.

AquaGuard AI addresses this by building predictive patterns mapping historical monthly precipitation cycles, annual total aggregations, and ambient cloud visibility indexes to output localized hazard calculations.

System Pipeline

Analytical Operations

  1. 1
    Data Ingestion & Cleaning: Fills null metrics using feature medians, filters duplicate values, and caps extreme outliers using IQR limits.
  2. 2
    Feature Standardization: Centers and scales measurements via StandardScaler so that differing numeric ranges do not skew distance-based functions.
  3. 3
    Multi-model Execution: Fits Decision Tree, Random Forest, KNN, and XGBoost structures on historical test fractions.
  4. 4
    Automated Selection: Extracts F1-Scores across structures to determine and save the best-performing model block.

Technologies and Core Libraries
